To the One Who Holds My Heart Angel,

In a world of endless faces, yours is the one that I cannot look away from.

You walk like poetry in motion, and my heart stumbles with every step you take.

I find myself captivated by the brilliance in your smile, as though the sun envies the light you carry within.

Every time you speak, it’s as if the universe quiets down to hear the music of your voice.

Your laughter lingers in my mind like the sweetest melody, replaying in moments when I need it the most.

I can’t help but wonder, do you know the magic you leave behind wherever you go?

You are the kind of rare beauty that artists dream of capturing and poets struggle to describe.

But it’s not just your grace that has me undone;

it’s the kindness in your eyes, the strength in your spirit, and the way you make the ordinary feel extraordinary.

If only you could feel what my heart whispers every time I see you.

It dreams of a world where you and I share sunsets, secrets, and something unshakable.

It longs to give you a love so steadfast, it could weather every storm.

You are my favorite thought, my sweetest daydream, my unspoken prayer.

If you take my hand, I promise to spend my life making sure you never doubt how cherished you truly are.

Yours, always hoping,

Macky Romero
